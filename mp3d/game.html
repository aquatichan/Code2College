HOW TO PLAY

🚗 Your car starts black and barely there. Bring it to life.

🎯 Neon markers rush toward you, each tagged with a key. Steer so the marker passes under your car, then tap its key right as it reaches you.

🌈 A perfect hit rotates your body color around the wheel and nudges alpha up. Early/late drains 5% alpha; a miss drains 15%.

🧱 Dodge the orange barriers — clipping one costs 15% alpha and 50 points.

💀 Hit 0% alpha and the car dissolves into the night and shatters.

🏁 Survive to the final note and blaze off into the sunset as a rainbow streak.

CONTROLS — click a key, then press a new one
Higher packs notes onto subtle sounds; lower keeps only the strong hits. Applies to AI & offline charts — re-analyzes on your next play.
