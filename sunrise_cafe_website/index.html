Our Story

Where Every Morning
Feels Like a Good One

Sunrise Cafe & Bakery was born from a love for slow mornings, buttery pastries, and the belief that great food brings people together. We set up shop on South Lamar to give Austin a neighborhood brunch spot that feels like home.

Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to craft croissants, cinnamon rolls, and banana bread from scratch. Our kitchen sources eggs, produce, and dairy from local Texas farms — because fresh ingredients make all the difference.
